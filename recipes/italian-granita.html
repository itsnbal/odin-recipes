Italian Shaved Ice Granita

Description

This frozen desert is perfect after a hearty valentine's day dinner.

Ingredients

  • lemonade
  • limoncello
  • fresh or frozen raspberries
  • zest of 1 lemon
  • raspberries and lemon zest, for serving

Steps

  1. Puree lemonade, limoncello, raspberries and lemon zest untli smooth in blender
  2. Pour into metal loaf, cover and freeze untli set, about 4 hours
  3. To serve, scrape surface of ice to create large flakes.
  4. Top with raspberries and zest, if desired
