Velvet Mug Cake

Description

A quick delicious cake with goey cream chesse hidden in its center for a most lovely valentine's day little surprise

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Granulated sugar
  • Unsweetened cocoa powder
  • Baking powder
  • Kosher salt
  • Whole milk
  • Unsalted butter
  • Vanilla extract
  • Red food coloring
  • Confectioners's sugar
  • Cream chesse

Steps

  1. In a mug, whisk the dry ingredients together.
  2. Stir milk, butter, vanilla and food coloring
  3. In a small bowl, mix confectioner's sugar and cream chesse until smooth
  4. Drop this into mug and push down until covered by batter
  5. Microwave on High until just cooked through
