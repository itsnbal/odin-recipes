Heart shaped cake

Description

A simple to make cake with a square and round baking pan to achieve all your valentine's baking dreams!

A perfect recipe to share with you significant other in this lovely day!

Ingredients

Cakes

  • flour
  • unsweetned cocoa powder
  • granulated sugar
  • packed brown sugar
  • baking soda
  • baking powder
  • salt
  • whole mil
  • canola oil
  • large eggs
  • pure vanilla extract
  • boiling water

Frosting

  • cream cheese
  • unsalted butter
  • confectioner's sugar
  • sprinkles and hearts for decorating!

Steps

  1. Heat oven. Coat square cake pan and round cake pan with nonstick cooking spray. Line bottoms with parchment
  2. In a large bowl, whisk dry ingredients
  3. In a medium bowl, whisk wet ingredients.
  4. Mix medium bowl in large bowl until fully incorporated. Mix boiling water
  5. Batter in pan. Cook 35 to 40 minutes.
  6. Let it cool in a wire rack
  7. Beat cream cheese and butter in a large bowl until smooth. Beat in conectioners's sugar until fluff, about 3 minutes
  8. Make the heart placing the square cake on a large board and orienting it like a diamond. Cut half of the round one. Stick the round halves to 2 sides of the square
  9. Decorate
